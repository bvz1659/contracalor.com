Description of Service

Contra Calor is a viticulture Growing Degree Day (GDD) tracking application that provides historical GDD accumulation data, Winkler scale classification, and vintage comparison tools to assist vineyard managers and wine industry professionals with phenological tracking and harvest planning.

GDD calculations are powered by the Visual Crossing Weather API using station-priority historical temperature data. Results are stored locally on your device as vineyard profiles with nested vintage records.

Free and Pro Tiers

Free Tier

The Free tier includes:

  • 1 saved vineyard profile
  • 1 vintage per vineyard (current growing season only)
  • GPS or manual map pin location
  • Current season GDD tracking with automatic daily updates
  • Winkler region classification

Pro Tier

The Pro tier is available by subscription and includes:

  • Unlimited vineyard profiles
  • Full vintage history per vineyard
  • 3-vintage comparison view
  • Southern Hemisphere support
